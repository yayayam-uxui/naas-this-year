## Creative-director production notes (must-read before voice generation)
1. **Bold close timing risk:** 12 words in a ~4s pocket — if crowded, start the close over the ✓✓✓ insert, or swap to the safe close (7 words). Generate the narrator at 2.4 AND 2.8 words/sec and compare; "I called back" needs a half-beat of air before the ring.
2. **"Still… conceptual?"** — the ellipsis IS the joke; verify the TTS holds the pause and rises on "conceptual".
3. **Band-pass reality check:** "Ninety minutes" / "Ten thousand meters" lose consonants under phone EQ — test the *filtered* audio, not the clean take.
4. **"She gets me." → RING** must be frame-locked to the beat — deliver the narrator stem dry; place the ring in the edit, don't bake it into the VO.
5. **Supportive Partner:** direct the read *down*, almost a whisper. It must not perform.
6. **Clearance rules baked in:** no "Instagram" on audio, on-screen apps generic/blurred; banned brand phrases appear only as villain push-props being swiped away; no real-person voice imitation.

- "Someone kept calling." — chosen independently by two writers; keeps the tagline fresh for the end card (3 of 4 writers flagged that the old line pre-spoke it).
