# NaaS — Nudge as a Service · Product Brief
_Working reference for every prompt in this project. Compiled 14 July 2026 from the team's WhatsApp thread (Mai's Gemini-assisted summary + Maya's English translation)._

> **Working name:** Nudge as a Service (NaaS) — temporary, final name + logo owned by Ido.

## One-liner
**Stop ignoring notifications. Start hitting your goals.**
NaaS replaces easily-ignorable lock-screen push notifications with active, direct, deeply personal **voice calls**.

## The problem
People are great at setting goals and making promises to themselves — and consistently fail at execution. Self-discipline is hard, and there is no external accountability when motivation fades.

## The ethos
Not nagging, not micromanaging, not petty. The app is a tough, highly dedicated **Executive Assistant**. The user is the Boss. Its job: push them upward, eliminate excuses, make sure they realize their full potential.
**Tagline energy: "We've got your back."**

## Differentiation — 4 Nudging Personas (chosen at onboarding)
| Persona | Voice | Example call |
|---|---|---|
| **Tough Love** | Blunt, provocative | "Hey loser, you promised you'd get up and run. Out of bed. Now." |
| **The Pragmatist** | Calm, objective, all business | "It is 6:30 AM. Your scheduled workout window is now open. Let's get to work." |
| **The Supportive Partner** | Empathetic, warm | "I know you're tired, but remember how good you'll feel after you finish this." |
| **Ego Pumper** | High-energy hype machine | "Hey champ! Your time to shine is right now. Go out there and crush it!" |
