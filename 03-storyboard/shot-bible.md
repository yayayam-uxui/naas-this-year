_8 shots × (start keyframe + end keyframe + video prompt). Built 15 July 2026, overnight session. Keyframes generated on Higgsfield Soul 2.0 (~0.12 credit/image) with a locked character reference; video prompts are self-contained (Kalshi method) and ready to paste into **Flow → "Frames to Video"** with the matching start/end images._

## Character lock (use in EVERY generation)
**The Boss:** woman, mid-20s, warm olive skin, shoulder-length dark curly hair, small silver hoop earrings. Wardrobe changes per scene; hair + face + earrings never change.
**The Friend:** NEVER shows her face — off-screen voice or back-of-head only (kills the second-character consistency problem).
**Apartment:** cozy urban living room, large window with city skyline view, warm wood + plants.
**Grade arc:** midnight teal + firework gold → grey daytime (the dead year) → steel-blue 5:59 → warming through the calls → full gold at the final NYE.
**Style tag (append to every image prompt):** cinematic film still, 35mm anamorphic look, shallow depth of field, subtle film grain, photorealistic.
**Video prompt rules:** self-contained (model sees nothing else), one camera move each, ambient audio only — **no dialogue, no music** (voices + music are laid in post).
